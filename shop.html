    We also have a small café attached to the farm shop so people can enjoy their purchases immediately At our farm shop, you’ll find a delightful selection of fresh, locally-sourced products to take home or enjoy on-site. Some of our most popular items include:

    • Fresh eggs – Straight from our free-range hens.
    • Milk – Creamy and wholesome.
    • Yogurt – Naturally delicious.
    • Cheese – Locally crafted and rich in flavor.
    • Bread – Baked daily, soft, and fragrant.
    • Cakes – Sweet and irresistible.

  • Welcome to our farm shop, your go-to destination for fresh, high-quality, locally-sourced products! Nestled in the heart of the farm, our shop is a haven for those who appreciate the taste of farm-fresh goodness. We pride ourselves on offering a carefully curated selection of items, including dairy products, baked goods, and more, all crafted with care and sustainability in mind.In addition to shopping, you can enjoy the convenience of our attached café, where you can sample your purchases in a warm and inviting atmosphere. Whether it’s the aroma of freshly baked bread or the rich flavors of handcrafted cheese, every visit to our shop promises a delightful experience for your senses. Come and discover the best of farm-fresh living with us!
